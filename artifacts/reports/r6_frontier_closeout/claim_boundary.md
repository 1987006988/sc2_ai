# R6 Claim Boundary

## Accepted

- replay-trained temporal opponent belief exists
- learned belief beats non-learning comparators on the accepted offline benchmark
- learned belief changes bounded online behavior on matched internal paired evaluation
- learned treatment has positive support on a narrow AI Arena-compatible local bot-vs-bot external slice
- the accepted claim remains within one learned feature plus bounded response surface

## Not Accepted

- broader external generalization
- third-party downloadable house-bot generalization
- medium-or-harder broad pool generalization
- ladder competitiveness
- second adaptive feature
- end-to-end self-play learning claim
