# R7 Claim Boundary

## Accepted

1. a strong-bot-anchored world-model line exists;
2. the learned advisor is not the downloaded substrate itself;
3. the learned advisor changes real macro behavior on the accepted strong substrate;
4. accepted offline, internal, and external evidence now all exist.

## Not Accepted

1. AI Arena top-bot level;
2. broad external bot generalization;
3. ladder competitiveness;
4. full causal counterfactual world-model supervision beyond proxy targets;
5. any claim that the downloaded substrate's native strength is our contribution.
